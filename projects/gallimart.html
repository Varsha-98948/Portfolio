GalliMart

A hyperlocal Android marketplace for ordering within 7 km.

Customer ordering flow
7 km location boundary
Seller inventory view

GalliMart treats local commerce like a product system: customers nearby, sellers nearby, and a clear operating radius that keeps the marketplace practical.

Why I Built It

To explore a real marketplace model with mobile ordering and local service constraints.

Real Problem

Small local sellers need discoverability and ordering flows that match nearby customer behavior.

Challenges

Balancing map behavior, range logic, seller workflows, and marketplace data.

Lessons Learned

Marketplaces require operational thinking beyond screens: supply, demand, location, and trust.
