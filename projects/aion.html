Meet Aion

Aion is currently evolving. A personal operating system for thoughts, memories, tasks, and projects — designed to bring your context back when you need it.

Development Preview

Aion is my attempt to make a calmer personal operating system: a place where scattered thoughts, project context, and next actions can come back when they are useful.

Why I Built It

I wanted a second brain that could organize ideas, memories, tasks, and projects while I learned AI by building something real.

Real Problem

Important context gets split across notes, chats, reminders, and memory. Aion explores how that context can become actionable.

Challenges

Learning AI while designing a product, keeping scope believable, and making the local portfolio version useful without external APIs.

Lessons Learned

Good Aion experiences need context, trust, memory, and restraint more than dramatic responses.
